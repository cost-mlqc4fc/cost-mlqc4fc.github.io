Workshops and Conferences

How MLQC4FC supports workshops and conferences

We support events in multiple ways, from fully Action-led initiatives to targeted grants for external conferences that align with our objectives.

Action events

Events fully organised by the Action and its Working Groups.

  • Thematic tracks led by Working Groups.
  • Hands-on sessions, hackathons, and benchmarking exercises.
  • Open calls for abstracts and demonstrations.
Co-organized events

Jointly organized workshops with partner networks and COST Actions.

  • Shared logistics, budgets, and scientific committees.
  • Integrated calls for contributions and speakers.
  • Co-branded dissemination and outreach.
Co-located events

MLQC4FC sessions hosted within larger international conferences.

  • Dedicated tracks or side meetings at major events.
  • Joint panels with aligned communities.
  • Visibility for Action results and collaborations.
Sponsored events

For events we do not organize, the Action can open a small grant call to support attendance.

  • Targeted grants for highly relevant conferences.
  • Selection based on Action priorities and impact.
  • Travel support for WG members and early-career researchers.
