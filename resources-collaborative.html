Collaborative tools

Spaces for coordination, messaging, and repositories

All Action collaboration tools live here: CERN e-groups for mailing lists and access control, Mattermost for WG discussions, Indico for events, and GitHub for shared repositories.

CERN e-groups

Mailing lists for general announcements, WG communication, and internal coordination.

  • General list: cost-ai-and-fc@cern.ch (self-subscription).
  • WG lists: members are added automatically after acceptance.
  • Internal lists: MC, Core Group, and cross-cutting committees.
Self-subscribe
Mattermost channels

Private channels for Working Group members, Core Group discussions, and cross-cutting coordination.

Open Mattermost
Indico pages

Central repository for event agendas, minutes, recordings, and shared materials.

Please notice that you can synchronize the parent, or any child category to your calendar through the dedicated “Export“ icon on the Indico page.
