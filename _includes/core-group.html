Core Group

The interface between all Action components

The Core Group connects the Management Committee and Working Groups, meeting regularly to monitor progress, prepare decisions, and react quickly to upcoming opportunities. It ensures that strategic choices are ready for MC approval and that the Action delivers on its milestones between plenary meetings.

See Upcoming Meetings
Core Group composition
  • Action Chair & Vice-Chair
  • Grant Manager and Science Communication Manager
  • Working Group Leaders & Vice-Leaders
  • Leads from Inclusiveness Target Country coordination and Young Researchers platforms
