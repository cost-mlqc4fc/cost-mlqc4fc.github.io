Action overview

MLQC4FC brings together five Working Groups, two cross-cutting committees, and a Management Committee coordinated by a Core Group. Delegates from COST Member Countries provide the mandate, the Core Group steers execution between plenary meetings, and each Working Group runs bottom-up initiatives aligned with the Action objectives.

The Grant Holder Team underpins everything with financial and administrative support. Together these layers keep the Action agile: WG deliverables flow into MC decisions, cross-cutting platforms provide resources for inclusiveness and communications, and the Core Group makes sure emerging opportunities are captured quickly.

Key building blocks
  • Management Committee – approves plans, budgets, and strategic directions.
  • Core Group – prepares MC decisions, monitors milestones, and maintains momentum.
  • Working Groups – deliver scientific outputs, benchmarks, and collaborative events.
  • Cross-cutting platforms – provide grant awarding and communication support.
  • Grant Holder Team – handles finances, reimbursements, and compliance.

Cross-cutting Platforms

Two cross-cutting committees keep the Action agile and inclusive. They connect Working Groups, the Core Group, and the Management Committee, translating strategy into hands-on support for members and stakeholders.

The platforms track Action-wide KPIs, coordinate joint initiatives, and ensure outcomes are visible both within the COST network and to external partners. They are also the main entry point for members seeking logistical guidance or communication support.

Management Committee

The Management Committee (MC) is composed of delegates nominated by COST National Coordinators. The MC oversees the Action strategy, approves activities, and ensures geographic balance across the network.

Visit COST Action Page
MC Responsibilities
  • Setting the scientific and networking roadmap.
  • Allocating resources to Working Groups and grants.
  • Monitoring progress and reporting to COST.
  • Coordinating with the Grant Holder institution and COST Association.
Core Group composition
  • Action Chair & Vice-Chair
  • Grant Manager and Science Communication Manager
  • Working Group Leaders & Vice-Leaders
  • Leads from Inclusiveness Target Country coordination and Young Researchers platforms

Core Group

The Core Group connects the Management Committee and Working Groups, meeting regularly to monitor progress, prepare decisions, and react quickly to upcoming opportunities. It ensures that strategic choices are ready for MC approval and that the Action delivers on its milestones between plenary meetings.

Contact the Core Group See Upcoming Meetings

Grant Holder Team

The Grant Holder Institution manages the Action’s finances, contractual obligations, and reporting to the COST Association. It supports Working Groups, the Core Group, and the MC with procurement, reimbursements, and compliance.

The team acts as the operational backbone of MLQC4FC: processing grants, assisting with event logistics, and ensuring every activity follows COST guidelines.

See COST rules Contact Grant Holder Team
Key responsibilities
  • Budget monitoring and financial reporting to COST.
  • Managing reimbursements for meetings, STSMs, and training schools.
  • Supporting dissemination, communication, and procurement activities.
  • Maintaining official records and liaising with the Grant Holder Scientific Representative.
