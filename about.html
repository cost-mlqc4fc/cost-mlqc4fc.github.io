Research Pillars

Working together across machine learning, quantum computing, and detector innovation

MLQC4FC builds bridges between disciplines that rarely meet in the same room. The Action mobilises expertise along four complementary axes that cover the full pipeline from theoretical ideas to deployable tools.

WG1 · Machine Learning

Designs robust ML pipelines for reconstruction, triggering, and analysis at future colliders, with emphasis on uncertainty quantification and real-time deployment.

WG2 · Quantum Computing

Explores quantum algorithms, simulators, and hybrid workflows that can accelerate optimisation, event generation, and pattern recognition tasks.

WG3 · Software & Data

Builds shared software stacks, benchmark datasets, and computing infrastructure guidelines so that new ideas can be validated and scaled quickly.

WG4 · Community & Outreach

Connects early-career researchers, promotes inclusiveness target countries, and communicates achievements to the broader collider and quantum technology communities.

Why join the Action?

Collaboration opportunities across Europe

MLQC4FC welcomes new members throughout the lifetime of the Action. Researchers from COST Member Countries, Near Neighbour Countries, and international partners can participate in Working Groups, submit proposals for Short-Term Scientific Missions, and help shape roadmaps for future collider facilities.

  • Access shared benchmarks, datasets, and demonstrators.
  • Apply for training schools, workshops, and networking grants.
  • Collaborate with cross-disciplinary teams tackling real detector challenges.
  • Contribute to the roadmap that informs Europe’s future collider strategy.

Get started

New participants are invited to browse the Working Group pages, connect with the Core Group, and follow announcements for upcoming calls.
