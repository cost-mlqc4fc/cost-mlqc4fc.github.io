Why join the Action?

Collaboration opportunities across Europe

MLQC4FC welcomes new members throughout the lifetime of the Action. Researchers from COST Member Countries, Near Neighbour Countries, and International Partner Countries can participate in Working Groups, submit proposals for Short-Term Scientific Missions, and help shape roadmaps for future collider facilities.

  • Access available tools, shared benchmarks, datasets, and demonstrators.
  • Apply for training schools, workshops, and networking grants.
  • Collaborate with cross-disciplinary teams tackling real detector and analysis challenges.
  • Contribute to the roadmap that informs Europe’s future collider strategy.
