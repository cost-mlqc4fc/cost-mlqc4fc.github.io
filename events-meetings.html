MC meetings

Held at least once a year.

  • Approve annual workplans, budgets, and major deliverables.
  • Review WG progress reports and grant awarding decisions.
  • Encourage cross-WG collaboration through plenary updates.

Core Group meetings

Held roughly every two months to maintain momentum.

  • Approve new WG applications.
  • Monitor milestones and prepare decisions.
  • Address operational issues quickly.
  • Coordinate with Grant Holder Team on logistics.

Focused sessions to review applications and prepare grant decisions.

  • Coordinate call timelines and feedback loops.
  • Ensure transparency and balance across WGs.
